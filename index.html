    Why?

    Ever since the virus originated in China, COVID-19 has spread rapidly around the world. Countries have been racing to contain the spread of the virus by enforcing curfews and quarantines. During these trying times, we noticed that misinformation spreads faster than the virus. That is why, we have decided to do our bit and designed the GoCorona bot to keep you updated on the latest news, statistics and notifications within the comfort of a conversation.

    How?

    The GoCorona bot was developed by AiBuddha using technology like Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning (ML). The bot brings you the latest updates on infection, death and recovery rates, availability of hospital beds in your city/state and updates and advisories from WHO and the government. GoCorona bot was created to debunk myths and will also provide useful tips to protect yourself and those around you.
